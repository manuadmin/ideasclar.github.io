About Company

IdeaScaler was created ground-up as an investment platform for small, disruptive ideas proposed by promoters. The theme is : bring an idea, back it up with passion and commitment and have the skills to exploit the ecosystem that IdeaScaler provides for accelerated value creation. We are based in the US and have offices in Canada, India and Thailand.

Who We Are

  • IdeaScaler is a studio investment fund which invests in referred ideas that can scale. The founders of IdeaScaler visualized a “referred investment” platform which funds ideas referred with, at best, one level of separation.

  • Ideas are aplenty; ideas that make a difference to the world and the investor are much fewer. It is the pedigree, the disruptive mindset and the energy behind the idea that gives it a fair shot in the low probability play of startups.

  • IdeaScaler invests in referred ideas in 4 key verticals- Digital Commerce, Consulting, Digital Insights and FinTech.

What We Do

IdeaScaler believes in investing in referred ideas- this is where LinkedIn meets money!

The emphasis is on founders with

  • One level of separation with the core investment group

  • Track record in the business to grow or disrupt traditional business models

  • Clean, professional, un-impeachable integrity

The above paradigm gives us unique positioning in the investment space.

Investment Philosophy

We are flexible in the areas we pick as long as it falls in the four broad categories. We also flexible on entry points on investments. But our philosophy is guided by what is famously called the Powell Doctrine which, in essence has three key aspects attributed to Colin Powell (Former Secretary of State of the USA).

Clear Objective

This would broadly translate to goals of the invested entity, our objectives for the investment, milestones and investment health measurements and contingency handling

Overwhelming Force

Make the investment count with support aspects provided by the IdeaScaler ecosystem. Multiple assets would be made available to the promoter to create an accelerated growth path for the invested entity. The combined efforts and resources would make the future happen faster!

Defined Exit

IdeaScaler is a not a “ cradle-to-grave” investment entity. Though we normally get in at different points in the start-up lifecycle, we clearly define exit points based on our investment objective for the specific invested entity. Our approach is to accelerate value creation and exit at the point of meteoric growth or penetration. Typically, this is the point of high investment demand which is beyond IdeaScaler’s investment range.
