Focus and Approach

Focus and Approach

Our focus in IdeaScaler is around making small investments, typically in early stage startups or those with a proven platform which need ecosystem intervention to take the game to the next level.

Verticals

IdeaScaler invests in four broad verticals- Fintech, Infotech, Pharma and Retail. Though Infotech is more a horizontal, our investments are focused on Infotech as a sector, and hence, we consider it a Vertical.

  • Fintech

    Primarily covering Payments, Microbanking, Audit and Credit Management

  • Infotech

    Primarily covering IT consulting, Candidate evaluation and validation

  • Pharma

    Primarily covering Medical Communication, Digital Analytics and eCommerce

  • Retail

    Primarily covering etail, device servicing community building and aggregation

Ecosystem

One of the key differentiators of IdeaScaler is our ability to provide an ecosystem to an invested start-up for accelerated value creation. IdeaScaler has a three-pronged strategy to ecosystem development- all concurrent and available to the startup at the time of its choosing.

This is how IdeaScaler looks at ecosystem support for a startup

Company

IdeaScaler was created ground-up as an investment platform for small, disruptive ideas proposed by promoters. The theme is : bring an idea, back it up with passion and commitment and have the skills to exploit the ecosystem that IdeaScaler provides for accelerated value creation.
